# Flying-Picasso
It flys and is made by me

This is a FPV drone 


GOAL:

. It flys, with a full range of motion and capability

. Has pre-set programs that with a pain brush, is able to draw designs

. Can go adequately fast, with a long lasting battery life


What is it?

The Flying Picasso is a custom made drone that has pre-made programs that makes the drone create drawings and designs on a canvas. 


Why did I make it?

I want to explore my engineering skills and complete something engineering-related for this summer. I think building a drone would be really cool.
